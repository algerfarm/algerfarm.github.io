Our Process

We take great care to make sure your puppy grows up happy and healthy.

We provide a health warranty for your puppy. We have never had a problem with any of our puppies, but we understand you are investing in a new companion and should receive reassurance of your purchase. We stand behind our breeding and puppies, and are positive that you will love your new dog and will enjoy a long and happy life with it!

  • Week 1: Puppies are born!

    Once the puppies are born, they spend a lot of time eating and sleeping. Their mother is fed 5-7 times per day with meals high in calories and calcium. This is done to ensure strong growth amongst the puppies and maintain the health of the mother.

  • Week 4:

    At 4 weeks, we begin weaning the puppies off of the mother. The puppies have started to develop their teeth at this point, and can begin moving towards more solid food. We will gradually introduce food (dry dog food soaked in water) and decrease the time with the mother.

  • More Week 4:

    We also begin deworming the dogs at 4 weeks with Ivermectin. We continue to administer small doses of deworming medication at 6 weeks and 8 weeks.

  • Week 5: Growing up!

    By week 5, the puppies have a lot more energy and are curious about the world around them. They spend more time outside running and playing. This is a great time to begin seeing their adorable personalities!

  • Week 8: Homeward bound!

    At week 8, the puppies will receive their first shot (administered by our local veterinarian) and total examination. You are welcome to take your puppy home anytime after they have turned 8 weeks

  • Questions?

    If you have any additional questions regarding the growth of your puppy, please feel free to email or call us. We are happy to help!

About Us

We are a young, recently married couple with our dog Coralee. She has become more than just our pet--she is family. We both have come from families that have owned several different breeds of dogs that were all unique in their own way, but none as much as our goldendoodle Cora. Micah takes her to work almost every day, and at night she cuddles up on the couch with me (Naomi). Since I have allergies to dogs, this has been particularly special to me.

Cora goes everywhere with us from hiking in the mountains to swimming in the lake. She loves to spend time with us and to be a part of our adventures. Cora has become a very personal, special part of our family. She picks up on our emotions, and often exemplifies the same ones to match. Cora gets incredibly excited when we come home--nothing stands in her way (not even a treat!) until we acknowledge her and show her love.

Ask anyone with a goldendoodle and they will tell you similar stories. Her calm demeanor, intelligence, and personality compare to none. We love our little addition to the family, and we hope you will find one for yours too!
